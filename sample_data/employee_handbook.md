# Employee Handbook — Nimbus Tech Pvt Ltd

## 1. Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday through Friday, with a one-hour lunch break.
Employees may opt into flexible hours (any 8-hour block between 7:00 AM and 9:00 PM) after completing
their probation period.

## 2. Leave Policy
- **Casual Leave (CL):** 12 days per year.
- **Sick Leave (SL):** 8 days per year, no medical certificate required for absences under 3 days.
- **Earned/Privilege Leave (EL):** 15 days per year, accrued monthly, carries forward up to 30 days.
- **Maternity Leave:** 26 weeks of fully paid leave.
- **Paternity Leave:** 2 weeks of fully paid leave.
- Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 3 working days in advance,
  except for sick leave, which may be applied for retroactively.

## 3. Probation Period
All new employees undergo a 6-month probation period. Confirmation is based on a performance review
conducted by the reporting manager in the 5th month.

## 4. Work From Home (WFH) Policy
Employees may work from home up to 8 days per month with prior manager approval. Engineering and
design roles may request a fully remote arrangement, subject to team lead and HR approval.

## 5. Code of Conduct
Employees are expected to maintain professionalism, avoid conflicts of interest, and report any
harassment or discrimination incidents to HR within 48 hours of occurrence. Nimbus Tech maintains a
strict zero-tolerance policy toward workplace harassment.

## 6. Reimbursement Policy
- Travel for official work is reimbursed at actual cost with receipts, capped at ₹3,000/day for local travel.
- Internet reimbursement for remote employees: ₹1,000/month.
- Reimbursement claims must be filed within 30 days of the expense.

## 7. Exit Process
Employees must serve a notice period of 60 days (30 days during probation). Full and final settlement,
including unpaid salary and leave encashment, is processed within 45 days of the last working day.

## 8. Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year — in April and October. Ratings range from
"Needs Improvement" to "Outstanding" and directly affect annual bonus payouts.

## 9. IT & Equipment Policy
Each employee is issued a laptop and standard peripherals. Loss or damage due to negligence is
chargeable to the employee at depreciated value. IT equipment must be returned at the time of exit.
