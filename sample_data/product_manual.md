# Product Manual — Nimbus SmartLock Pro

## 1. Overview
The Nimbus SmartLock Pro is a Wi-Fi and Bluetooth-enabled smart door lock supporting PIN codes,
fingerprint unlock, mobile app access, and physical key backup.

## 2. Box Contents
- SmartLock Pro unit
- Mounting plate and screws
- 2x physical backup keys
- 4x AA batteries
- Quick start guide

## 3. Installation
1. Remove the existing deadbolt and measure the door thickness (35–45mm supported).
2. Attach the mounting plate using the provided screws.
3. Insert the 4 AA batteries into the battery compartment on the interior unit.
4. Align and screw in the exterior and interior units, connecting the internal cable.
5. Download the "Nimbus Home" app and scan the QR code on the back of the lock to pair it.

## 4. Connecting to Wi-Fi
Open the Nimbus Home app → Settings → Network → select your 2.4GHz Wi-Fi network and enter the
password. The SmartLock Pro does NOT support 5GHz networks.

## 5. Adding Users
Up to 50 users can be added via the app. Each user can be assigned a unique PIN (4–8 digits) or
have their fingerprint enrolled directly on the lock's scanner (up to 20 fingerprints total).

## 6. Battery Life & Low Battery Alerts
Battery life is approximately 9–12 months under normal use. The lock emits 3 beeps and the app
sends a push notification when battery level drops below 20%.

## 7. Auto-Lock Feature
Auto-lock can be configured in the app to automatically lock the door 10, 30, or 60 seconds after
it is closed. This feature is disabled by default.

## 8. Troubleshooting
- **Lock not responding:** Replace batteries; if issue persists, hold the reset button for 10
  seconds to restart the unit.
- **Wi-Fi won't connect:** Confirm you are using a 2.4GHz network; move the router closer if signal
  is weak (lock requires at least -70dBm signal strength).
- **Fingerprint not recognized:** Clean the sensor with a dry cloth and re-enroll the fingerprint.

## 9. Warranty
The SmartLock Pro comes with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Warranty
does not cover water damage or damage from forced entry attempts.
