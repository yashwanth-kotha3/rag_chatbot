# Frequently Asked Questions — Nimbus Tech Pvt Ltd

**Q: How many casual leaves do I get per year?**
A: Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves per year, as outlined in the leave policy.

**Q: Can I work from home permanently?**
A: Fully remote arrangements are only available to Engineering and Design roles, and require
approval from both the team lead and HR.

**Q: What is the notice period for resignation?**
A: The standard notice period is 60 days. During probation, the notice period is reduced to 30 days.

**Q: How do I claim travel reimbursement?**
A: Submit your travel receipts through the HR portal within 30 days of the expense. Local travel
reimbursement is capped at ₹3,000 per day.

**Q: When are performance reviews conducted?**
A: Performance reviews happen twice a year, in April and October, and influence annual bonus payouts.

**Q: What happens if I damage my company laptop?**
A: If the damage is due to negligence, the depreciated value of the equipment will be charged to
the employee. Accidental wear and tear during normal use is not chargeable.

**Q: How long is maternity leave?**
A: Maternity leave is 26 weeks, fully paid. Paternity leave is 2 weeks, fully paid.

**Q: Who do I contact if I experience workplace harassment?**
A: Report the incident to HR within 48 hours. Nimbus Tech enforces a zero-tolerance policy on
workplace harassment.
